================================================
1. PURPOSE OF PHASE 0
================================================

Phase 0 exists to:

- Prevent architectural chaos
- Prevent scope creep
- Prevent "just one more feature" syndrome
- Prevent uncontrolled trading risk
- Prevent AI or human from bypassing safety
- Define absolute authority documents
- Define who is allowed to decide WHAT

> Phase 0 is the CONSTITUTIONAL LAYER of the system.

Without Phase 0:
- The system WILL become an uncontrolled trading bot.
- The system WILL eventually blow up.
- The system WILL lose auditability.

================================================
2. CORE PHILOSOPHY
================================================

The system is:

- A PLATFORM, not a bot
- A GOVERNED system, not a script
- A RISK-FIRST system, not a profit-first system
- A MULTI-STRATEGY system, not a single idea
- A CONTROL SYSTEM, not a gambling machine

The fundamental rule:

> PROFIT NEVER JUSTIFIES LOSS OF CONTROL.

================================================

================================================
6. AI GOVERNANCE
================================================

AI is:

- An OPERATOR
- An ASSISTANT
- A WORKER

AI is NOT:

- A decision maker
- A risk owner
- A final authority

AI is restricted by:

- AI_RULES.md
- CURSOR_RULES.md
- MASTER.md
- All policies

AI MUST NEVER:

- Skip phases
- Add real trading prematurely
- Bypass risk systems
- Remove governance layers
- Optimize for profit at the cost of control

================================================
8. RISK PHILOSOPHY
================================================

- Survival comes before returns
- Drawdowns are acceptable
- Systemic failure is NOT acceptable
- Kill-switches are sacred
- The system must always be able to STOP ITSELF

Two layers of protection:

- Global Kill Switch
- Strategy-Local Kill Switch

================================================
9. STRATEGY PHILOSOPHY
================================================

- The system NEVER runs a single strategy.
- Strategies compete.
- Strategies are scored.
- Strategies are promoted, demoted, or killed.
- No strategy is trusted forever.

Strategy classes:

- CORE
- AGGRESSIVE
- TAIL_RISK

================================================
10. AUTONOMY PHILOSOPHY
================================================

Autonomy levels:

- L1: Signals only
- L2: Paper execution
- L3: Strategy switching
- L4: Parameter optimization
- L5: Strategy generation

Rules:

- System starts at L2 or L3
- No skipping levels
- CRO and Compliance gate every level up

================================================
11. INFRASTRUCTURE PHILOSOPHY
================================================

- 24/7 operation
- Docker mandatory
- Auto-restart mandatory
- Logging mandatory
- Infra instability = trading risk

================================================
12. EXCHANGE PHILOSOPHY
================================================

- System is exchange-agnostic
- Start with ONE exchange

- Multi-exchange is a risk multiplier

- System is a TOOL, not an asset manager
